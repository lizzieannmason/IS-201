My Favorite Styles

  1. Bohemian (Boho-Chic)
    • Boho Chic incorporates fluid silhouettes, mixed textures, earthy color palettes and global patterns.
    • It values comfort, nautral materials and individuality. Often accesories are the star.
    • Favorite brand for this style is Free People.
  2. European Street Style
    • Europeans are masters at mixing luxury designer pieces with affordable high-street finds or vintage items.
    • This style is much more "classy" incorperating neutral & monochromatic palettes with taylored fits.
    • Favorite brand for this style is Zara
